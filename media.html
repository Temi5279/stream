Media Usage Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

1. Third-Party Media Integration

Stream integrates with these media platforms to enhance your experience:

Video Hosting Services

  • Vimeo: For premium content delivery
  • YouTube: For supplemental video content
  • Brightcove: For live streaming events

These services may collect usage data per their own policies.

Social Media

  • Facebook/Instagram: For sharing content and authentication
  • Twitter: For real-time updates
  • TikTok: For short-form video previews

Social features are optional and require explicit user consent.

2. Embedded Content

We may display content from other websites:

  • News clips
  • Trailers
  • User-generated content (with permission)

This content behaves as if you visited the original site (may collect data).

3. Media Licensing

All integrated media is properly licensed:

  • Commercial streaming rights for all videos
  • Royalty-free or licensed music
  • Proper attribution for creative commons content

4. User-Generated Content

For channels allowing uploads:

  • Must own rights or have permission
  • No copyrighted material without authorization
  • We may remove infringing content

5. Data Collection by Third Parties

External services may collect:

  • Device information
  • Viewing metrics
  • Location data (for regional content)

See each platform's privacy policy for details.

6. Your Controls

You can:

  • Disconnect social media integrations
  • Opt-out of non-essential embeds
  • Report unauthorized content
