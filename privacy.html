Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect information to provide better services to our users:

  • Account Data: Email, username, and profile information when you register
  • Usage Data: Content watched, search queries, and interactions
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies for login and preferences
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by our payment partners (for premium accounts)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Deliver personalized content recommendations
  • Improve and optimize our streaming service
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Prevent fraud and ensure account security
  • Communicate service updates and offers
  • Analyze usage patterns for business insights

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Aggregated Data: Non-identifiable usage statistics with advertisers

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication options
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure with firewalls

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data removal
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Cookies: Manage preferences in browser settings
  • Data Portability: Export your watch history

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through email or in-app notifications. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@stream.com
