Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my video buffering?

Buffering can occur due to internet speed fluctuations. Try:

  • Checking your internet connection (minimum 5Mbps recommended)
  • Reducing video quality in settings
  • Restarting your router
  • Closing other bandwidth-intensive applications
How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel:

  1. Go to Account Settings
  2. Select "Subscription"
  3. Click "Cancel Plan"
  4. Follow the confirmation prompts

Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Which devices are supported?

Stream works on:

  • Smartphones/Tablets (iOS & Android)
  • Web Browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Android TV)
  • Streaming Devices (Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV)
  • Game Consoles (PlayStation, Xbox)
How do I reset my password?

Password reset steps:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your account email
  3. Check your email for a reset link
  4. Create a new strong password
