1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. We use them to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Personalize content

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

Required for basic functionality:

  • User authentication
  • Session management
  • Security features

Performance Cookies

Help us improve our service:

  • Page load times
  • Error tracking
  • Feature usage

Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience:

  • Language preferences
  • Player settings
  • Regional content

Advertising Cookies

Used for:

  • Ad personalization
  • Campaign measurement
  • Frequency capping

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari etc.)
  • Our cookie consent manager (below)
  • Third-party opt-out tools

4. Third-Party Cookies

These services may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Traffic analysis
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Vimeo: Video playback

5. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of significant changes. Continued use implies consent.
